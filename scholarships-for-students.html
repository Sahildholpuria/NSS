Types of Scholarships

Academic Excellence

For students who have demonstrated outstanding academic performance. This scholarship rewards dedication to studies and hard work.

Financial Aid

For students from low-income backgrounds who need financial assistance to pursue their education. This scholarship helps cover tuition and other educational expenses.

Special Needs Scholarships

For students with special needs, this scholarship provides financial support to help students access quality education in an inclusive environment.

How to Apply

Step 1: Choose Your Scholarship

Select the scholarship you wish to apply for based on your eligibility.

Step 2: Fill Out the Application Form

Complete the online application form with your personal and academic details.

Step 3: Submit Required Documents

Upload necessary documents such as your academic records, financial information, or medical certificates if applicable.

Step 4: Wait for Results

After submitting your application, wait for the results and further instructions.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a full-time student enrolled in an accredited educational institution.
  • Must meet the minimum GPA requirement (varies by scholarship type).
  • For financial aid scholarships, household income should fall below a specified threshold.
  • Special needs scholarships require appropriate medical documentation and proof of eligibility.
